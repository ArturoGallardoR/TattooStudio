Frequently Asked Questions

Does getting a tattoo hurt a lot?

Pain is subjective, but our artists use techniques to minimize it and most clients report only mild discomfort.

What is the minimum age to get a tattoo or piercing?

The legal minimum age is 18 with valid ID. We do not tattoo or pierce minors without legal consent.

How do I take care of my new tattoo?

Keep it clean, avoid sun exposure, don't scratch it, and follow the artist’s instructions for proper healing.
